Non Vegatarian Lunch

Sweet & Spicy Turkey Sandwich

Ingredients:

2 slices bread, 4 slices roasted turkey breast, 1 slice pepperjack cheese, 2 teaspoons butter, 4 teaspoons strawberry preserves

Directions:

Heat a small skillet over medium heat. Butter one side of each of the bread slices with one teaspoon of butter. Place one slice, butter side down, in the skillet. Top with the turkey and cheese slices. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter side up. When the first side of the sandwich is golden brown, turn and brown the other side, 3 to 5 minutes per side, or until the cheese begins to melt. Remove the sandwich to a plate and top with strawberry preserves or serve the preserves on the side.

Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:

One dices, fresh onion, one cup of cooked, diced, chicken, One box of Aneto Natural Chicken Broth, One cup of frozen mixed vegetables, One cup of egg noodles.

Directions:

Start by simply sautéing your onion it a bit of olive oil (okay, you caught me….you need some oil for this recipe) Add chicken, vegetables and broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for about 10 minutes then add noodles and cook according to their package directions or until al dente. Season to taste with salt and pepper (although that Aneto broth is so flavorful you won’t need much salt) and add a sprinkle of fresh or dried parsley as a garnish. This recipe serves four and makes an ideal lunch or dinner

Tuna-Egg Salad Sandwhich

Ingredients:

Bread, canned tuna, large eggs, green onion, mayonnaise, salt

Directions:

Bring a pot of water to boil. Carefully lower eggs into the hot boiling water. Reduce to a rolling simmer on medium heat and boil eggs for 10 minutes uncovered. Prepare an ice bath and transfer the hard-boiled eggs into the ice water to cool completely. Use the back of the spoon to crack and peel the eggs. Place peeled eggs in a large bowl. Add strained tuna, green onions, mayonnaise, salt and black pepper. Mash everything together using a fork. Spread the tuna egg mayo mixture on a slice of bread or toast. Optional: add lettuce on top. Cover with another slice of bread or toast.

Lasagna

Ingredients:

2 Tbsp. olive oil, 2 Large cloves garlic, finely chopped, 1 lb. ground pork, salt and pepper, 1/3 cup tomato paste, 3/4 cup dry white wine, 3/4 cup roughly chopped flat-leaf parsley,1 15-oz container part-skim ricotta, 1/4 cup plus 2 Tbsp grated pecorino, 4 cups baby spinach, roughly chopped, 4 oz. part-skim mozzarella, 6 no-boil lasagna noodles

Directions:

1. Heat oven to 425°F. Heat oil and garlic in a large skillet on medium. Add pork, season with 1/4 tsp each salt and pepper, and cook, breaking sausage up with a spoon into very small pieces, until beginning to brown, 5 to 6 min. Add tomato paste and cook, stirring, for 2 min. Add wine and simmer for 1 min. Fold in ¼ cup parsley. 2. Meanwhile, in a bowl, combine ricotta, ¼ cup pecorino, and ¼ tsp each salt and pepper. Fold in spinach, all but ½ cup mozzarella, and remaining ½ cup parsley. 3. Spread 1/2 cup sauce on bottom of 8-in. square baking dish. Top with 2 noodles and spread one-third (about 1/4 cup) of remaining sauce over the top. Dollop with one-third (about 1 cup) of ricotta mixture; repeat. Place remaining 2 noodles on top; spread with remaining sauce and dollop with remaining ricotta mixture. 4. Sprinkle with remaining ½ cup mozzarella and 2 Tbsp pecorino, cover tightly with an oiled piece of foil (to prevent sticking) and bake 15 min. Uncover and bake until noodles are tender and top is golden brown, 8 to 10 min.
