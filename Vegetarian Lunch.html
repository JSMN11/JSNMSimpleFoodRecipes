Veggie Burrito

Ingredients:

Avocado, Cilantro, Lime, Sour Cream/Greek Yogurt, Black Beans, Rice, Tortilla, Jalapeño Peppers and Onions

Directions:

Blend avocado to a creamy sauce, using avocado, cilantro, jalapeño, garlic, lime juice, and sour cream or Greek yogurt Make the cilantro-lime rice, using a small pan, and combine dry rice with 2 cups of water and 1/2 tsp salt. Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low, and wait until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is tender Prepare fajita veggies, while the rice cooks heat 2 Tbsp. of oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Once hot, add bell peppers, poblano pepper, and red onion. Cook 7 to 9 minutes, stirring only occasionally, until tender Layer the vegetables, rice, and cream sauce, squeeze some lime over it, and wrap the tortilla with the mixture.

Spaghetti

Ingredients:

A box of spaghetti, Marinara sauce, Two large pots, and Salt Water

Directions:

Fill up a large pot with water at about ¾ the way, add a pinch of salt, and boil. Once the water is boiling, add the spaghetti. You should cook it for 7-11 minutes. Depending on when done, you strain the spaghetti Add marinara sauce to your strained spaghetti, mix them well, and serve!

Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

Slices of bread, Butter, Cheese, Optional: Mayonnaise

Directions:

Optional: Spread Mayo on the outside of 2 slices of bread. Add the grated cheese evenly across the inside. Choose a medium sized pan and turn the flame on to medium. Add butter to the bottom of the pan and wait until it is melted. Add the sandwich to the pan and cover it with a lid. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bread is golden brown. Flip and cook, covered, on the second side When the bread is brown on both sides and the sandwich is full of melted cheese, remove it from the pan and serve!
